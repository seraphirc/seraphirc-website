SeraphIRC

A new client for people who never left IRC.

SeraphIRC runs on desktop, iPhone, and iPad. It supports IRCv3 and connects through Soju, ZNC, or directly to an IRC network. Your chat history and configuration stay on your own devices.

SeraphIRC on desktop and mobile

The desktop app is a full graphical client with a network tree, channel buffers, and user lists. The mobile app puts the same networks on your phone, with touch navigation and push notifications.

Features

  • Multi-network IRC: keep direct networks, bouncer imports, channels, server buffers, and private messages organized in one workspace.
  • First class Soju support: import networks from a Soju bouncer, discover bouncer networks automatically, and manage them without leaving the client.
  • ZNC and direct IRC: connect through an existing ZNC setup, or talk directly to traditional networks when you don't run a bouncer at all.
  • IRCv3 negotiation: advertised capabilities such as SASL, server-time, account tags, away-notify, echo-message, batch, and chat history.
  • SASL and NickServ: log in the way established networks expect registered users to — SASL during connection or a classic NickServ identify.
  • Local history and search: search recent conversations while keeping history and configuration under local user control.
  • Themeable interface: a prebuilt collection of common, beloved themes ready to use.
  • Diagnostics built in: inspect connection behavior, server responses, and client state without guessing what happened.
  • Nothing phones home: no analytics, no account requirement, no cloud dependency. The client talks to your networks and nothing else.
  • Desktop and mobile: take the desktop's familiar interface layout and flows with you on the go with your mobile device.
  • Mobile push notifications: receive push notifications on your mobile device when the setting is enabled on your app.
  • File uploads: use your Soju bouncer to upload files and share with others.
  • Emoji support: display emojis and use the emoji picker to spruce up your chat.
  • Voice dictation: use your mobile device's microphone to translate your voice to IRC message. Handy for responding on the go.

Made for Soju, works with ZNC and direct connections

If you keep IRC running through a bouncer, SeraphIRC understands that setup natively. If you don't, direct connections behave like any classic client.

  • Soju: import networks straight from the bouncer and manage bouncer-aware connections inside the client, including push notifications over soju.im/webpush.
  • ZNC: existing setups keep working, including command forwarding and the usual user/network style of login.
  • Direct networks: no bouncer? Connect straight to a network and everything works as you'd expect: channels, services, SASL, the lot.
